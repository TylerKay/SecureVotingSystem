U.S. Elections - Electronic Voting System


Constitutional Amendments


Failure to vote on a constitutional amendment will have the same effect as voting no on the amendment.

To vote for a proposed constitutional amendment, select the "YES" option on that question.
To vote against a proposed constitutional amendment, select the "NO" option on that question.
